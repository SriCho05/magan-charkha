# **App Name**: Khadi Kraft

## Core Features:

- Product Browsing: Browse Khadi products, sorted by category.
- Product Details: View individual product details including description and images.
- Shopping Cart: Add products to cart and proceed to checkout.
- Product Filtering and Sorting: Sort/filter products by color, type, and price.
- Product Management: Manage product inventory including adding, updating, and deleting items (admin-only).
- Order Management: View all orders and manage fulfillment (admin-only).
- AI Product Suggestions: AI tool suggests related products to customers while browsing based on item descriptions and user history.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Earthy Olive Green (#8B864E) to evoke a natural, organic feel.
- Background color: Light Beige (#F5F5DC) providing a neutral, calming backdrop.
- Accent color: Warm Brown (#A67B5B) to highlight important elements.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, is suitable for comfortable reading of descriptions and details. Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern sans-serif with a high-end feel.
- Use simple, line-based icons that represent categories, actions, and elements of the site to complement the minimalist style.
- Employ a clean and minimalist layout with plenty of whitespace to focus attention on the products. Grid layout to display product listings effectively.
- Use subtle transitions and animations, like a fade-in effect for loading images, to enhance user experience without being distracting.
